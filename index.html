What MiSizura does

Everything you need to find your fit

From a quick size lookup to full measurement-based matching, MiSizura covers every type of shopping — online and second-hand.

🤖

AI Size Finder

Enter your measurements once. The AI Size Finder searches hundreds of brand size charts and returns your predicted size for that specific brand — not a generic conversion.

⭐

My Accurate Sizes

Browse all the brands MiSizura knows about and see your predicted size for each one at a glance. Filter by category, fit type, height type and region.

👙

Bra Hub

Filter bra brands by band size, cup size and fit type (Standard, Sports, Plus, Maternity) to instantly see which brands carry your exact bra size across all regions.

👟

Shoe Hub

Find shoe brands that stock your size in UK, US, EU or AUS sizing. Separate sections for women's and men's footwear with full foot-length reference data.

🏊

Swimwear Hub

Dedicated swimwear sizing by brand — because swimwear sizing is notoriously inconsistent. Filter by fit type and region to find the right brand for your shape.

📊

MiStats & Weight Tracking

Log your weight over time and visualise your journey. MiStats shows your progress with a clean graph, start/current/target weights, and total change at a glance.

👤

Multiple Profiles

Create profiles for yourself, friends or family — each with their own measurements, body type and weight log. Perfect for shopping as a gift too.

☁️

iCloud Sync

All your profiles and weight entries sync automatically across your iPhone, iPad and other Apple devices via iCloud — no account needed.

🌍

8 Global Regions

Switch between UK, US, EU, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, China and All Regions. Size displays adapt to your chosen region throughout the entire app.

Getting started

Up and running in minutes

No account, no sign-up — just open the app and start finding your size.

1

Create a Profile

Add your gender, name, and body measurements — waist, bust/chest, hips and height. You can also set your height type (Regular, Tall, Petite) and fit type.

2

Choose a Region

Select your preferred size region (UK, US, EU, etc.) and the app will display all sizes in that format throughout.

3

Pick a Brand or Category

Use AI Size Finder to look up a specific brand, or browse My Accurate Sizes to see all your fits at once. Filter by clothing category to narrow it down.

4

See Your Size

MiSizura shows your predicted size along with a match score and measurement details, so you can judge how confident the recommendation is.
